Features

Great features you'll love.

We believe in providing its users with a smooth-easy going process for sharing and borrowing books. Here we present the features of our services.

Book Share

This is an online portal that allows users to exchange books or use either one of the option between sharing or borrowing. Portal allows users not only to borrow a book, but also to search for books that are available for borrowing in their preferable genre and provides opportunity to find some new good books.

User Credibility

User's credibilty is built up based on his/her bahavior. Users with more share and responsible (delays and damage free) return of books have more credibility. User can also earn points by solving Crosswords and puzzles which will also add to their credibility.

Get Notifications

In case of unavailablity of a particular book, user can mark the book in wish list and he/she will get notification on its availability.In case of multiple requests, priority is given based on credibility of borrower.

Gifts and Prizes

Based on credibility, some selected users will be given chapter samples of the New Books of Publication Houses.

Damage Cover

The sharing portal includes Damage cover E-bond between borrower and sharer to be paid by borrower in case of damage or delay so as to uphold decorum and dignity of website.

Social Media Reach

Social Media outreach of the user also add up to credibility and shows trust a person can have. This portal makes use of this and thus, also maintains wide social outreach.
