Features

Great features you'll love.

We believe in providing its users with a smooth-easy going process for sharing and borrowing books. Here we present the features of our services.

Book Share

This is an online portal that allows users to exchange books or use either one of the option between sharing or borrowing. Portal allows users not only to borrow a book, but also to search for books that are available for borrowing in their preferable genre and provides opportunity to find some new good books.

User Credibility

User's credibilty is built up based on his/her bahavior. Users with more share and responsible (delays and damage free) return of books have more credibility. User can also earn points by solving Crosswords and puzzles which will also add to their credibility.

Get Notifications

In case of unavailablity of a particular book, user can mark the book in wish list and he/she will get notification on its availability.In case of multiple requests, priority is given based on credibility of borrower.

Gifts and Prizes

Based on credibility, some selected users will be given chapter samples of the New Books of Publication Houses.

Damage Cover

The sharing portal includes Damage cover E-bond between borrower and sharer to be paid by borrower in case of damage or delay so as to uphold decorum and dignity of website.

Social Media Reach

Social Media outreach of the user also add up to credibility and shows trust a person can have. This portal makes use of this and thus, also maintains wide social outreach.

Faq

Questions and Answers.

What is the guarantee of my book being returned unharmed?

Borrower signs an e-bond with the website as well as the user to return the book unharmed and without delay. In case of damage or delay, borrower will be liable to appropriate charges.

What benefits do I get by sharing my book?

Sharing a book will lead to increase in credibility which will give you priority when you are in need of a book.Along with that, most credible users gets free coupons and chapter samples of upcoming books from Publication houses associated with our website.

Where can I earn points from?

Daily crosswords and puzzles are available on the websites, which on solving gives you points. Also, your timely return of books without any damages adds points to your profile which can be redeemed in various ways.

How can use the points I earned?

The points earned by you add upto your credibility and gives you priority during borrowing a book. You can also use these points to pay for your delay or damage charges.

Do you provide delivery services for books?

We follow a very transparent process in the whole sharing and borrowing scenario. From the time we recieve a donor-reciever match, we make arrangments for collecting and delivering the book timely.

Why should I sign-up with my facebook account?

Signing in with facebook leads to increase in credibility and builds up the trust among users and their validation. However, it is not mendatory, you can also sign up using mail. We ensure no personal information is used without user's permission. For more details please read our Terms and Condition about user information.
